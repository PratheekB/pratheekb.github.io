About

Pratheek is a Ph.D. candidate at the Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru. His current research revolves around improving GPU memory management, focussing on address translation and data placement for large memory workloads. His work spans the domains of GPU micro-architecture, compiler techniques, and system design. Previously, he had worked on reverse-engineering Nvidia GPUs. He also maintains an active interest in CPU micro-architecture research.
