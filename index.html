Ph.D. Candidate, Computer Science, IISc
Bio

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru specializing in GPU memory management, micro-architecture, and system design. My work focuses on address translation, data placement for large-memory workloads, and the intersection of architecture and systems. Previously, I worked on reverse engineering Nvidia GPUs and remain active in CPU micro-architecture research.
